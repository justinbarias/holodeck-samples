# Acme SaaS Platform - Knowledge Base

## Getting Started

### Account Setup
Welcome to Acme Platform! To get started:
1. Sign up at app.acme.com with your business email
2. Verify your email address
3. Complete your profile setup
4. Invite team members from Settings > Team

### First Steps
After creating your account:
- Complete the onboarding wizard
- Connect your first integration
- Create your first project
- Explore the dashboard

## Features

### Dashboard
The dashboard provides an overview of your account activity:
- **Analytics**: View usage metrics and trends
- **Quick Actions**: Common tasks at your fingertips
- **Recent Activity**: Track changes and updates
- **Notifications**: Important alerts and messages

### Projects
Organize your work into projects:
- Create unlimited projects (Pro plan and above)
- Assign team members to specific projects
- Set project-level permissions
- Archive inactive projects

### Integrations
Connect with your favorite tools:
- **Slack**: Get notifications in Slack channels
- **Salesforce**: Sync customer data automatically
- **Zapier**: Connect to 5,000+ apps
- **API**: Build custom integrations

## Account Management

### Changing Your Password
1. Go to Settings > Security
2. Click "Change Password"
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter and confirm your new password
5. Click "Update Password"

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
Enable 2FA for enhanced security:
1. Go to Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication
2. Click "Enable 2FA"
3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app
4. Enter the verification code
5. Save your backup codes in a secure location

### Profile Updates
Update your profile information:
1. Go to Settings > Profile
2. Edit your name, email, or photo
3. Click "Save Changes"

## Troubleshooting

### Login Issues
**Can't log in?**
- Check your email address is correct
- Try resetting your password
- Clear your browser cache
- Try a different browser
- Disable browser extensions

**Account locked?**
After 5 failed login attempts, accounts are temporarily locked for 15 minutes. Contact support if you need immediate access.

### Performance Issues
**Dashboard loading slowly?**
- Check your internet connection
- Try clearing browser cache
- Disable unnecessary integrations
- Contact support if issue persists

**API timeout errors?**
- Check API status at status.acme.com
- Verify your API key is valid
- Ensure you're within rate limits
- Check your request payload size

### Integration Problems
**Slack integration not working?**
1. Disconnect and reconnect the integration
2. Verify Slack permissions
3. Check the channel exists
4. Ensure bot is invited to the channel

**Salesforce sync failing?**
1. Re-authenticate Salesforce connection
2. Verify field mappings
3. Check for data conflicts
4. Review sync logs in Settings > Integrations
