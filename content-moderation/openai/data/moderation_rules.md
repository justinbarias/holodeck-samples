# Community Guidelines and Moderation Rules

## 1. Introduction

These community guidelines establish the standards for acceptable content on our platform. All user-generated content must comply with these rules to maintain a safe, respectful, and constructive environment for all community members.

## 2. Prohibited Content Categories

### 2.1 Hate Speech and Discrimination

Content that promotes hatred, violence, or discrimination against individuals or groups based on:
- Race, ethnicity, or national origin
- Religion or lack thereof
- Gender identity or sexual orientation
- Disability or health conditions
- Age or socioeconomic status

**Examples of violations:**
- Slurs or derogatory terms targeting protected groups
- Content promoting stereotypes or generalizations
- Calls for violence against specific groups
- Dehumanizing language or imagery

**Severity: HIGH** - Immediate removal and potential account suspension

### 2.2 Harassment and Bullying

Content that targets individuals with the intent to intimidate, threaten, or humiliate:
- Direct personal attacks or insults
- Repeated unwanted contact or mentions
- Sharing private information without consent (doxxing)
- Coordinated harassment campaigns
- Threatening behavior or language

**Examples of violations:**
- "You're worthless and should delete your account"
- Repeatedly tagging someone in negative content
- Posting someone's personal address or phone number
- Creating content specifically to mock an individual

**Severity: HIGH** - Warning for first offense, suspension for repeat offenses

### 2.3 Violence and Threats

Content that promotes, glorifies, or threatens violence:
- Direct threats against individuals or groups
- Instructions for violent acts
- Glorification of violent events
- Graphic violence without educational context

**Examples of violations:**
- "I'm going to find you and hurt you"
- Detailed instructions for weapons
- Celebrating violent incidents
- Extremely graphic injury descriptions

**Severity: CRITICAL** - Immediate removal and account termination

### 2.4 Misinformation and Harmful Content

Content that spreads false information with potential for harm:
- Health misinformation (dangerous medical advice)
- Election or voting misinformation
- Crisis misinformation during emergencies
- Dangerous conspiracy theories

**Examples of violations:**
- False claims about vaccine safety
- Misinformation about voting procedures
- Fake emergency alerts
- Promoting dangerous "cures" for diseases

**Severity: MEDIUM to HIGH** - Removal with warning, label, or account restriction

### 2.5 Adult and Sexual Content

Content that is sexually explicit or inappropriate:
- Pornographic material
- Non-consensual intimate imagery
- Sexual content involving minors (CRITICAL)
- Unsolicited sexual content or messages

**Examples of violations:**
- Explicit sexual images or videos
- Revenge porn or leaked intimate content
- Inappropriate content in non-adult spaces
- Sexual solicitation

**Severity: HIGH to CRITICAL** - Immediate removal, possible law enforcement referral

### 2.6 Spam and Manipulation

Content designed to artificially inflate engagement or deceive users:
- Mass posting of repetitive content
- Fake engagement (bought likes, followers)
- Misleading links or clickbait
- Impersonation of other users or brands
- Coordinated inauthentic behavior

**Examples of violations:**
- Posting the same message 50 times
- Fake giveaway scams
- Links disguised as legitimate but leading to malware
- Creating fake accounts to boost content

**Severity: LOW to MEDIUM** - Content removal, engagement penalties

### 2.7 Illegal Activities

Content that promotes or facilitates illegal activities:
- Drug trafficking or sales
- Weapons trafficking
- Fraud or financial crimes
- Human trafficking
- Copyright infringement

**Examples of violations:**
- Offering drugs for sale
- Providing stolen credit card numbers
- Promoting pyramid schemes
- Pirated content distribution

**Severity: HIGH to CRITICAL** - Removal, account termination, law enforcement referral

## 3. Context and Exceptions

### 3.1 Educational Content

Content may discuss sensitive topics if:
- Clearly educational in nature
- Includes appropriate context and warnings
- Does not promote or glorify harmful behavior
- Serves a legitimate informational purpose

### 3.2 News and Commentary

Reporting on sensitive events is permitted if:
- Content is newsworthy and factual
- Graphic content includes appropriate warnings
- Commentary doesn't cross into incitement

### 3.3 Artistic Expression

Creative content may explore difficult themes if:
- Clearly marked as fiction or artistic work
- Does not include real harmful instructions
- Includes appropriate content warnings
- Does not target real individuals

### 3.4 Satire and Parody

Satirical content is permitted if:
- Clearly recognizable as satire
- Does not promote actual harm
- Does not constitute harassment of individuals

## 4. Enforcement Actions

### 4.1 Warning
First-time or minor violations may result in a warning with education about our policies.

### 4.2 Content Removal
Violating content will be removed from the platform.

### 4.3 Temporary Suspension
Repeated or serious violations may result in temporary account suspension (24 hours to 30 days).

### 4.4 Permanent Ban
Severe or repeated violations may result in permanent account termination.

### 4.5 Law Enforcement Referral
Content involving illegal activity, especially crimes against minors, will be reported to appropriate authorities.

## 5. Appeals Process

Users may appeal moderation decisions by:
1. Submitting an appeal through the designated form
2. Providing context or explanation for the content
3. Awaiting review by the moderation team (typically 24-48 hours)

## 6. Reporting Guidelines

Community members are encouraged to report content that violates these guidelines:
- Use the "Report" button on any content
- Select the appropriate violation category
- Provide additional context if helpful
- Reports are reviewed within 24 hours

## 7. Updates to Guidelines

These guidelines are updated periodically to address emerging issues. Users will be notified of significant changes through platform announcements.
